Ogundiran and Partners

At Ogundiran and Partners, we believe in the power of the law to make a positive impact on society. As a part of our commitment to social justice and community service, we are proud to offer pro bono legal services. Our pro bono practice is dedicated to providing high-quality legal assistance to those who may not have the means to afford it, ensuring access to justice for all.

Our Pro Bono Commitment

Our firm recognizes the importance of giving back to the community and supporting individuals and organizations in need. Through our pro bono program, we offer free legal representation and counsel in various areas of law, including but not limited to:

  • Human Rights: Advocating for the rights of marginalized and vulnerable groups, including victims of human rights abuses and discrimination.
  • Public Interest Litigation: Engaging in cases that address broader societal issues, such as environmental protection, public health, and consumer rights.
  • Access to Justice: Providing legal aid to individuals facing eviction, unfair treatment, or other legal challenges that impact their basic rights and livelihoods.
  • Nonprofit Support: Assisting nonprofit organizations with legal matters related to their operations, governance, and compliance.

Our lawyers are passionate about using their legal expertise to effect positive change and support the underprivileged. We work closely with clients to understand their unique situations and provide compassionate, dedicated representation.

Eligibility and Application

Our pro bono services are available to individuals and organizations that meet specific criteria, typically based on financial need and the nature of the legal issue. To apply for pro bono assistance, prospective clients are encouraged to contact our office and complete a brief intake process. Our team will assess each request to determine eligibility and the best way to provide support.

We collaborate with various nonprofit organizations, legal aid societies, and community groups to identify and reach out to those in need. Our goal is to ensure that everyone has access to legal representation, regardless of their financial circumstances.

Our Impact

Ogundiran and Partners takes pride in the positive outcomes we have achieved through our pro bono work. From securing justice for individuals facing discrimination to supporting charitable organizations in their mission to improve society, our pro bono practice has made a meaningful difference in the lives of many.
